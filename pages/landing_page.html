Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Ayurvedic consultations

Ayurveda has been practiced for over 5000 years with proven effectiveness in treating chronic conditions, stress-related disorders, and lifestyle diseases. While conventional medicine excels at acute care and emergencies, Ayurveda addresses root causes and provides long-term solutions. Many patients find success with Ayurveda after conventional treatments haven't worked. Our approach is evidence-based and combines ancient wisdom with modern scientific validation.

Results vary based on your condition and commitment to the treatment plan. Most patients notice improvements within 4-6 weeks, with significant changes by 8-12 weeks. Acute conditions may respond faster, while chronic issues require more time for complete healing. Unlike quick-fix medications, Ayurveda focuses on sustainable, long-term wellness. We provide regular progress assessments and adjust treatments as needed.

Ayurvedic treatments are completely safe when prescribed by certified practitioners. We use only high-quality, tested herbal formulations with no synthetic additives. Unlike pharmaceutical medications, Ayurvedic remedies work with your body's natural healing mechanisms and have zero side effects. All our practitioners are certified by recognized Ayurvedic institutions and follow strict safety protocols. We also ensure our treatments don't interfere with any existing medications you may be taking.

Yes, absolutely. We work collaboratively with your existing healthcare providers and never recommend stopping prescribed medications without medical supervision. Ayurvedic treatments can complement conventional medicine and often help reduce dependency on medications over time. During your consultation, we'll review all your current medications and create a treatment plan that works safely alongside them. Many patients gradually reduce their medication needs as their health improves, always under proper medical guidance.

Your first consultation is a comprehensive 60-minute session where we'll conduct a thorough health assessment including pulse diagnosis, tongue examination, and detailed discussion of your health history, lifestyle, and concerns. We'll identify your unique body constitution (Prakriti) and current imbalances (Vikriti). You'll receive personalized recommendations for diet, lifestyle, and herbal remedies. We'll also answer all your questions and create a customized treatment plan. Come prepared with a list of current medications and any recent medical reports.
